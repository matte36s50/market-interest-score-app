About the Market Interest Index (MII)

Formula Weights

  • Sale Price — 30%
  • Bid Activity — 30%
  • View Count — 20%
  • Comments — 12%
  • Social Engagement — 5%
  • Vehicle Age — 3%

Confidence Levels

  • ● High: 50+ auctions
  • ◐ Medium-High: 20–49
  • ◐ Medium: 10–19
  • ○ Low: 5–9 auctions

Data Sources

MII scores are calculated from auction data aggregated from Bring a Trailer and Cars & Bids. Refreshed twice daily at 6 AM and 6 PM UTC.
