Manufacturer Apex Index (MAI)

MAI measures manufacturer presence and performance at apex classic car auction events. It proxies the network density (D) term in the Networked Utility Dividend framework: U = (Fun × Exp) + Nostalgia + (CulturalSalience × Nα × D) × (1/Survivorship)

Q — Quality
Mean(sold price / high estimate) for sold apex lots — measures price realisation

Sell-through rate for apex lots — measures market confidence

About the Market Interest Index (MII)

Formula Weights

  • • Sale Price: 30%
  • • Bid Activity: 30%
  • • View Count: 20%
  • • Comments: 12%
  • • Social Engagement: 5%
  • • Vehicle Age: 3%

Confidence Levels

  • ● High: 50+ auctions
  • ◐ Medium-High: 20-49 auctions
  • ◐ Medium: 10-19 auctions
  • ○ Low: 5-9 auctions

Data Sources

MII scores are calculated from auction data aggregated from Bring a Trailer and Cars & Bids. Data is refreshed twice daily at 6 AM and 6 PM UTC.
